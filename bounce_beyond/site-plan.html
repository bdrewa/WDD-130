Overview

Purpose

Bounce and Beyond offers fun and reliable party rentals, including lawn games, bounce houses, DJ services, and elegant wedding equipment and decoration rentals. Our mission is to make every event, whether playful or formal, memorable and stress-free with high-quality service and gear.

Audience

Our target audience includes parents, schools, churches, and community event organizers who are planning parties, carnivals, or other special occasions. We also serve soon-to-be newlyweds, couples planning weddings, and anyone who wants a professional DJ or MC to bring their event to life. They are seeking trustworthy equipment, excellent customer service, and a memorable experience for guests of all ages.

Content

Home page

Welcome to Bounce and Beyond. Bounce and Beyond is a family-owned business built on fun, trust, and community. We are a young couple with a baby, proud to carry on a legacy that began over 20 years ago with our great-uncle. For two decades, he ran a respected local business that provided party rentals, bringing joy to countless families and community events. Now, we're taking the torch and expanding the vision, adding new services while keeping the same dedication to quality and service that made the business thrive. Our mission is simple: to help families, friends, and communities create memorable events without stress. Whether you're planning a birthday party, school carnival, family reunion, or wedding, we're here to make your celebration unforgettable. We offer reliable, safe, and fun equipment with friendly, family-centered customer care. As a family, we recognize the significance of these moments and the challenges they present in terms of organization. That's why we treat every event as if it were our own, offering helpful guidance, flexible scheduling, and a personal touch that you won't find with big rental chains. We're not just a business. We're part of your community, and we're here to help you bounce beyond expectations.

Rentals & Services

What We Offer. At Bounce and Beyond, we specialize in creating fun, flexible, and functional event experiences for all ages. Our rental options include two bounce castles to suit different event needs. We offer a small bounce house perfect for backyards, toddlers, or more casual gatherings and a large, commercial-grade inflatable ideal for bigger parties, school events, and community festivals. Both are cleaned and inspected after every use for your safety and peace of mind. But we don't stop there. We're expanding our services to include full wedding equipment rentals and professional DJ services. If you're planning a wedding and need tables, chairs, arches, lighting, or decor, we have curated options to help you create a beautiful setup without the hefty price tag. We want to bring the same ease and joy to your special day as we do to our bounce house rentals. And for the ultimate party experience, you can now book our wedding DJ service for just $500. We offer a complete sound system, curated playlists, event coordination, and an energetic host to keep the celebration going. Whether you need soft background music or a packed dance floor, we're ready to help bring your vision to life. No matter what you're celebrating, we're here to make it special.

Booking & Contact

Book With Us. Booking with Bounce and Beyond is simple, affordable, and stress-free. We understand that your time is valuable, and we've designed our process to be as straightforward as possible.To reserve a bounce house, wedding rental, or DJ service, please don't hesitate to contact us through our website's contact form, send us a message on social media, or call us. We'll get back to you quickly with availability and any follow-up questions. We provide delivery and setup for all bounce house rentals, ensuring everything is securely set up and ready before your event begins. Do you need a recommendation on where to place it or how much space it requires? Just ask. We're happy to help. We're flexible, responsive, and committed to making your celebration a success. Whether you're planning months or looking for a last-minute booking, we're here for you. Let's make your next event one to remember. Get in touch today!
