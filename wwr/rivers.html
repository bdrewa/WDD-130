Explore Our Rivers

Mad Ride White Water Rafting offers unforgettable experiences on some of the most scenic and thrilling rivers in the country. Whether you're looking for a gentle float or white-knuckle rapids, we’ve got the perfect river adventure for you.

  • Snake River – Gentle waters, great for families and first-timers.
  • Colorado River – A perfect balance of fun and adventure.
  • Salmon River – Our wildest ride for experienced rafters.

Each trip is led by our professional guides and includes safety gear, training, and a whole lot of fun. Come make memories with us!
